# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Be respectful.
- Be constructive.
- Help new contributors.
- Accept feedback professionally.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Personal attacks
- Spam
- Malicious contributions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove inappropriate contributions or comments.

Let's build a helpful and respectful security community.
